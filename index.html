Turret Alarm Clock

Arduino - Mechanics

The Turret Alarm Clock is a playful but aggressive solution to oversleeping. The system rotates a turret toward the user, plays custom audio and forces interaction in order to silence the alarm.

Technical overview

  • Microcontroller: Arduino
  • Actuators: stepper motor for rotation, servo for elevation
  • Audio: DFPlayer Mini with speaker
  • Sensing: limit switches or sensors to calibrate position
  • Extras: load cell for future interaction or detection

What I learned

  • Coordinating multiple actuators smoothly
  • Designing a clear UX for a physical device
  • Debugging timing issues and mechanical play

Aquaponics - L'espoir du Nord

Research - Sustainability

A series of aquaponics systems designed and tested to explore food production in Northern contexts. The project includes water quality monitoring, cycling optimization and communication with the public.

System & methodology

  • Fish tanks and grow beds with controlled flows
  • Tracking nitrogen cycle with nitrifying bacteria
  • Excel based models to compare system parameters
  • Experimental setups with different species and layouts

Impact & outreach

  • Presented at Expo sciences Hydro Québec
  • Featured at science festivals and research events
  • Helped demystify sustainable technology for visitors

Manège tes méninges

3D printing - Client work

Manège tes méninges is a board game used by the Cégep de Jonquière. I designed and 3D printed custom pieces, from trees to ride components, working with staff to match their needs and budget.

Design & fabrication

  • CAD modeling of trees, gears and ride elements
  • Iterating dimensions for reliable printing and assembly
  • Batch printing and quality control for class use

Professional skills

  • Managing expectations and timelines with clients
  • Documenting parts for future reprints
  • Estimating time and material costs for a contract
